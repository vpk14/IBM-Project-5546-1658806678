Food Bot

Nutrition is a critical part of health and development. Better nutrition is related to improved infant, child, and maternal health; stronger immune systems; Here, the Food Bot helps in a way to analyze the nutritional content of the food that is prepared in real time. For now, the bot works in aspects of analyzing the nutritional facts of fruits, and further development leads to the analysis of other dishes and food items. To help this bot, one can upload images of their daily food item via the app, which automatically does the data crunching and returns a table with nutritional facts and other required details in an easy-to-read format, for example.
